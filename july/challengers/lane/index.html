Cold-High July

The 60-second challenge that makes you feel alive!

While highlighting you just how comfortable your life is and reminding you you always have the choice...

Challenge 1 — A Coldie

Have a 60 second cold shower!

This challenge is made for everyone. Anyone can do it, including you.

  • By the end of July have a 60 second cold shower
  • You have the entire month of July to practice
  • Start by having 5-10 second cold showers
  • Work up to 20-30 second cold showers
  • Stretch yourself to 45 seconds
  • Finally, a 60 second Coldie
  • Every try's a Cold-High!

Note: In this challenge you can start and finish your shower with hot water, and add your Coldie somewhere in between

Need something more challenging? There are other Cold-Highs to try...

Challenge #2 — Daily Coldies

Have a 60 second cold shower every day!

This challenge is for those who think just one 6o second cold shower in July is too easy. Anyone can do this too, including you.

  • Have a 60 second cold shower every day of July
  • You have 31 minutes of will power don't you?
  • You'll get 31 Cold-Highs this July
  • Every try's a Cold-High!

Note: In this challenge you can start and finish your shower with hot water and add your 60 second Coldie somewhere in between.

Want to challenge your own inner-Wim? Try Pure Cold...

#3 Pure Cold

Have 100% cold showers in July

This challenge will test the best of you. Anyone can do this, including you.

  • Have 100% cold showers for the month of July
  • No hot, just cold
  • 100% cold
  • 100% of July
  • Every try's a Cold-High!

Note: In this challenge you start and finish your shower with cold water and you don't touch the hot tap anywhere in between. This challenge is not timed to 60 seconds — your entire shower is cold. 100% cold. Pure cold.

Hi I'm Lane and I've been cold showering on-and-off for a little while now. Inspired by Wif Hof — thanks to this VICE documentary — the benefits regular Cold-Highs bring to my mental health and motivation levels are so easy to see: and they really do make me feel alive! So I'm challenging myself to a Cold-High July and inviting everyone to join me. I'm going Pure Cold which means I'll have 100% cold showers this month. I live in Melbourne too where it's Winter, so arrh! It will be a challenge, and that's the point.
